About me

Hey, I am Finn. Currently, I am looking for Ph.D. positions in Deep (Reinforcement) Learning and am working towards completing my Master's Degree in Computer Science at the University of Hamburg, Germany. I've got a Bachelor in Human-Computer Interaction, which is an interdisciplinary study path, seeking to combine cognitive principals from Psychology with Computer Science, for Interface Design.

Though, during the three years of completing that degree, I've developed a greater interest in other areas of Computer Science, mainly in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics and Data Science, which leads me to this website.

About this site

This sites is subject to mainly two ideas:
Firstly, I want to contribute to the community and share knowledge about topics I care about. I have read many great blogs and tutorials, but also others that didn't delight me as much. I try to build upon that, and fill gaps where I experience a lack of material or documentation. However, you might also find me writing about topics that are already well covered, in which case I simply wanted to take my spin on a topic ;)
This addresses the blog posts you find on this website, which relate to the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Neural Networks, Python programming and Linux Sysadmin tools.

Secondly, this website serves as my digital business card. Hence, you find some of my projects on here as well. These aim at illustrating a part of my skill-set, and unlike the blog posts, they are not ment to be educational resources.

Cheers, Finn Rietz.
